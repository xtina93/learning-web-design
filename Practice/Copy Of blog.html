Low and Slow

posted by BGB, November 15, 2012

This week, I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacumm-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low temperature poaching.
